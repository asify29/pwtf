About Pure Water Foundation

Founded with a vision to bring clean water to every corner of Gilgit-Baltistan, the Pure Water Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to improving the health and well-being of the region's communities.

Why We Exist

In Gilgit-Baltistan, many rural communities rely on contaminated streams and rivers for their water supply. This leads to widespread waterborne diseases, affecting children's health, school attendance, and overall community well-being. We aim to tackle these challenges by implementing clean water projects that are sustainable, community-driven, and effective.

OUR VISION

To create a world where every individual in Gilgit-Baltistan has access to clean, safe, and reliable drinking water.

OUR MISSION

To provide sustainable clean water solutions, educate communities on water management, and foster health and well-being through access to pure water.

MISSION STATEMENT

At Pure Water Foundation Gilgit-Baltistan, our mission is to transform lives by delivering sustainable clean water solutions to the remote regions of Gilgit-Baltistan. We believe that access to clean water is a fundamental human right, and we are committed to ensuring that every household, school, and healthcare center has access to safe drinking water.

OUR PROJECTS

Our Impact: Bringing Clean Water to Gilgit-Baltistan

1. Community Wells and Boreholes

We dig deep wells and boreholes in villages with no access to clean water, providing safe drinking water to hundreds of families. Our wells are equipped with hand pumps, ensuring that water is accessible year-round.

2. Water Filtration Systems

We install water filtration systems in schools and healthcare centers to provide clean, safe water for students and patients. These systems remove harmful contaminants, ensuring a healthier future for our communities.

3. Rainwater Harvesting

In regions where water scarcity is a challenge, we implement rainwater harvesting solutions to capture and store rainwater for community use, especially during dry seasons.

4. Hygiene and Sanitation Education

Beyond just providing water, we believe in empowering communities through education. We conduct workshops on hygiene, sanitation, and water conservation to promote healthier living practices.
